The Artist

Meghana Govindaraj

Dentist turned Pastry Chef

In a unique twist of fate, Meghana swapped her dental drill for a piping bag, bringing surgical precision to the art of French Patisserie.

Alumni of Whitecaps International School of Pastry. Based in Bangalore, India.

What began as a passion for science in dentistry evolved into a love for the chemistry of baking. At Magnum Patisserie, she combines exactitude with artistic flair, proving that the most beautiful smiles are the ones created by a perfect dessert.
